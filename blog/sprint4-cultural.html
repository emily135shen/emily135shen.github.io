How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

How the concept of neuroplasticity may benefit individuals? If you don’t know already neuroplasticity is the concept of one’s brain being able to restructure itself, throughout an individual’s life.

“The brain can, and does change throughout our lives. It is adaptable, like plastic hence why neuroscientist call this neuroplasticity”.

This process may be used when areas of an individual’s brain is damaged (left hemisphere and right hemisphere) and thus repairing some speech lost. Repairing the individuals brain also depends on an individual’s genetics, the outcome of this is how well the brain will be able to restructure itself. How does this work? The process of rewiring our brain creates new connections and weakens the old connections this process is neuroscience in action. Practicing an activity or accessing a memory is part of the process of rewiring our brain. This allows our brains to be reshaped to the activity or memory being practiced.

How might engaging with the principals of neuroplasticity be useful to your own benefit

Engaging with the principals of neuroplasticity maybe useful to my own benefit because I can engage in healing, rewire my brain to be positive, or train my brain to improve my focus.
