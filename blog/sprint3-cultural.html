EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE BLOG

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is us as humans having the potential to understand others. There are 4 traits in the emotional intelligence. These include self-awareness, self-management, Empathy and social skills.

Self-awareness: Being able to understand your emotions and thoughts. As well as understanding, how to control the negative emotions. And allowing ourselves to recognise what the cause is that triggers the negative emotions. This being feelings are connected to how we behave.

Self-management: Being able to redirect your negative emotions into positive outcomes. Having self-control of your negative emotions or bad habits. Being able to adapt to new changes in life. And being able to be open to new ideas.

Empathy: Understanding other people’s feelings, thoughts and behaviour. As well as being able to relate and understand other people’s experiences.

Social skills: Being able to understand others around us allows us to have a solid foundation to build and maintain positive relationships.

How Is it different to IQ?

SEQ is emotion based whereas IQ is intelligence based.

Why is it important?

EQ is important because it effects our lives in a positive way by helping us become better decision makers. As well as helping us develop better social skills and makes us better leaders.
